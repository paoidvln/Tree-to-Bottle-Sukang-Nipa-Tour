The Ingredient

What is
Sukang Nipa?

Sukang Nipa is a natural vinegar crafted from the fermented sap of the nipa palm — one of the Philippines' most treasured coastal plants. It is a staple in Filipino cuisine, prized for its distinctively sour taste, natural origins, and time-honored health benefits.

Rooted in the traditions of Pangasinan, this vinegar represents more than flavor — it embodies community, heritage, and a deep respect for the land.

About the Tour

A Cultural &
Eco-Tourism
Experience

The Sukang Nipa Eco-Tour Experience is a hands-on cultural and eco-tourism activity that showcases the traditional process of making Sukang Nipa. This tour supports local farmers while preserving Filipino heritage and championing sustainable practices.

Designed for students and curious travelers alike, every stop on the itinerary connects participants to the land, the people, and the living traditions of Pangasinan.
